MeLOne.r2_score — Method
r2_score(y_real, y_pred)

Computes the R² score given by $1 - ∑(yᵢ - ŷᵢ)² / ∑(yᵢ - ȳ)²$, where y is y_real, ŷ is y_pred and ȳ is the mean of y_real.
